About Our Company

Kunlad Properties was incorporated in 2015. The real estate firm was formed to carry out a well detailed property management which entails rents / debts recovery, facility management, feasibility and viability studies.

Kunlad Properties is a pre-eminent focused real estate consultant with a large, well balanced portfolio comprised of residential, commercial and mixed-use properties. The Company also has a small but growing portfolio of property investments in Nigeria and an interest in property development across borders.

We've also served as residential and industrial Property Management Specialists; where we've gained good reputation in property management; because we deliver a service that provides integrity, professionalism and peace of mind to our clients.

It is the policy of KUNLAD PROPERTIES to conducts its activities in such a way and manner that continuously minimizes emissions, effluents and discharges of materials that are known to degrade the environment.


Management is aware that to achieve these goals, it has the responsibility to provide effective supervision, training and monitoring programs.

Employees as part of their responsibilities are also required to conduct themselves and the job in a manner that will have minimal degrading effect on the environment.

The ultimate objective of this policy is to completely eliminate all negative effects on the environment by virtue of our services.

The policy is established as part of the overall KUNLAD PROPERTIES.
